## Questions & Answers

**Q: Will this hurt our original featured link (linkedinpreview.com)?**
A: No. Our tool is different enough with unique features. We're ranking on our own merits for different keywords.

**Q: Should we remove the reference to linkedinpreview.com?**
A: No. It's open source so we can acknowledge the original. Just differentiate clearly.

**Q: How long until we see results?**
A: Weeks 1-4: Eliminate duplicate content penalty. Weeks 4-8: Begin ranking for new keywords. Weeks 12+: Establish authority.

**Q: What if users don't want to sign up for accounts?**
A: Core preview tool works without signup. Accounts are optional for save/share features. Lower friction = better conversion.

**Q: Should we A/B test messaging?**
A: Yes, after launch. Test different headlines, CTAs, feature descriptions. Use analytics to optimize.
